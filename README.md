# Customer_behavior_analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
An end-to-end data analytics project that analyzes customer demographics and transaction patterns to uncover actionable insights for revenue growth and targeted marketing. This project covers the full lifecycle: **Data Preprocessing (Python) ➡️ Advanced Querying (SQL) ➡️ Interactive Dashboarding (Power BI)**.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Skills
*   **Data Cleaning & EDA:** Python (Pandas, NumPy)
*   **Database Querying:** SQL
*   **Data Visualization:** Power BI
*   **Reporting:** Markdown, Jupyter Notebooks

---

## 📂 Repository Structure
*   📁 `python_learning.ipynb` — Data auditing, cleaning, and Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA).
*   📁 `customer_behavior.sql` — SQL scripts for metric extraction and business KPIs.
*   📁 `Customer_Behavior_Dashboard.pbix` — Interactive Power BI visual dashboard.
*   📁 `Customer-Shopping-Behavior-Analysis.pdf` — Comprehensive executive summary report.
*   📁 `customer_shopping_behavior.csv` — Raw source dataset.

---

## 🔄 Data Pipeline & Analytics Process

### 1. Data Prep & Exploratory Analysis (Python)
*   Handled data quality issues by managing missing values, formatting inconsistencies, and isolating outliers (IQR method).
*   Analyzed demographic correlations (Age, Gender, Geography) against purchasing trends to build early operational hypotheses.

### 2. Strategic Querying & Business Logic (SQL)
*   Aggregated metrics to track **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)** and transaction frequency.
*   Engineered customer segmentation rules based on purchase frequency and overall spend metrics.
*   Isolated high-performing product categories and prioritized payment distribution patterns.

### 3. Executive Dashboarding (Power BI)
*   Developed a dynamic interface tracking critical business KPIs: Total Revenue, Active Customers, Average Purchase Ratings, and Retention Rates.
*   Designed visual pathways for regional distribution maps, multi-variable trend lines, and categorical spending cross-sections.

---

## 📊 Business Insights & Recommendations
*   **Targeted Demographics:** Identified top-performing age segments and their preferred digital payment methods to optimize checkout experiences.
*   **Promotional Impact:** Quantified how promo codes boost transaction volume versus their net impact on average order values.
*   **Action Plan:** Recommended highly automated marketing tracks tailored around high-margin categories during identified peak transaction hours.
